All in one

Mercury includes radar, minute-by-minute, 48 hour forecast, and 7 day general forecasts. Just tap on an hour to reveal more information, and tap on a day to reveal the information for that day.

Features

Rain Radar

Weather radar for all across the globe, every 10 minutes. Use the map in the app to see your location and many others.

Minute-by-minute

See if it will rain for the next hour with the minute-by-minute forecasts in the app. Minutely data presented as a bar chart so you know exactly when it will rain.

48 Hour Forecast

The next 24 hours are displayed on the home page, with the option to load 24 more. Tap on an hour to reveal more nuanced details about the hour.

Place Search

Find places by keyword, address, or region. Then get all the same information for that place that you can get at your location

7 Day Forecast

A simple presentation of a 7 day forecast, with a tap to reveal hourly data for the next 48 hours after that day.

Customization

Customization options like custom themes, app icons, taptic feeback customizer, and a radar color customizer, you can make your forecast the way you want.
