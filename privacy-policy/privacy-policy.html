Mercury Privacy Policy

Mercury is a privacy-driven weather app. When opening the app, it will request access to your location so that we can access the weather data for where you are. This location is sent to the Dark Sky API (recently purchased by apple), and their privacy policy can be found here. The radar data is also pulled from the RainViewer API, and their privacy policy can be found here. Any settings that are stored are only stored on the device, and no data is sent to any external servers. The data you generate on the device stays on the device, and that will never change. If you have questions please contact me at ryanmon731@gmail.com
